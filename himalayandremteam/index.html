HIMALAYANDREAMTEAM

Nepal is home to the Himalayas, the highest mountain range in the world. But besides mountains, the country has to offer a very ancient, fascinating and still vibrant culture in which old traditions and folklore define life in cities and villages. It is the land of colors and scents, the land of mystery and magic. That’s why Nepal is for travel. Himalayandreamteam offers customized and ready-made holiday packages for everyone: individuals, couples, families or groups. We carefully guide each trip with the professionalism and passion we have been known for almost ten years.
